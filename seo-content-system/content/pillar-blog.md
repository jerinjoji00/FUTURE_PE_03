## Core Digital Marketing Services Offered by Agencies in Kochi

### 1. Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

SEO is the process of improving your website's visibility in organic (unpaid) search engine results. A professional SEO strategy includes:

- **Keyword research** – identifying the terms your customers are searching for.
- **On-page optimization** – optimizing title tags, meta descriptions, headings, and content.
- **Technical SEO** – improving site speed, mobile-friendliness, and crawlability.
- **Link building** – earning backlinks from authoritative websites.
- **Local SEO** – optimizing your Google Business Profile for local searches.
